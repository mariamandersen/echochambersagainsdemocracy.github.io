Algorithm / Transparency Slider

Ask questions and turn the slider to change how the agent frames the topic.

Manipulating→Open / topic transparent
Use the Transparency slider to control how the agent frames responses — from manipulative (low) to topic‑transparent and reflective (high). Responses are text‑only.
Tip: move the slider between questions. At low transparency the agent nudges you; at high transparency it explains the topic and asks what you think.
